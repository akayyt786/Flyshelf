# 🥇 PocketShelf

### *The Magical, On-Device AI Shelf for macOS*

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
[![Swift](https://img.shields.io/badge/Swift-5.9-orange.svg)](https://swift.org)
[![macOS](https://img.shields.io/badge/macOS-14.0+-blue.svg)](https://apple.com/macos)

**PocketShelf** is a premium, open-source floating shelf for macOS designed to hold your files, links, and snippets in 100% private, on-device storage. Inspired by utilities like Dropover and Yoink, PocketShelf outranks the competition by integrating **Apple's Neural Engine** for local intelligence without any cloud dependencies.

---

## ✨ Features

*   **🪄 Shake-to-Open**: Move your mouse rapidly while dragging a file to spawn an instant shelf near your cursor.
*   **🧠 Process 100% On-Device**:
    *   **Smart Renaming**: Using local OCR (Vision) and NLP to suggest descriptive names for your files.
    *   **Semantic Organization**: Automatically categorize your stashed items into projects, documents, or media.
*   **💻 Keyboard-First Workflow**: Full support for `CMD+C`/`CMD+V` and arrow-key navigation for power users.
*   **🌑 Notch Interaction**: Drag files near your MacBook notch to reveal a visual glow and drop target.
*   **📂 Multi-Shelf Management**: Create and manage multiple independent shelves simultaneously across workspaces.
*   **🛠️ Contextual Smart Actions**: Remove backgrounds from images, convert to PDF, or merge documents—all locally.

## 🚀 Installation

### Direct Download
1.  Download the latest release from the [Releases](https://github.com/armankatia/pocketshelf/releases) page.
2.  Drag `PocketShelf.app` to your `/Applications` folder.

## 🔒 Privacy

PocketShelf is built with privacy as a first-class citizen. 
*   **No File Data Leaves Your Mac**: All AI processing (Renaming, Sorting, OCR) happens locally on the Apple Neural Engine.
*   **Zero Tracking**: No analytics, no accounts, no cloud sync.
